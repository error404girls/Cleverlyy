About

Cleverly is an intelligent web app designed to help students study more efficiently and retain more information in less time. Simply by uploading the study material — whether it's text, a PDF, or even an image — the AI automatically generates concise, high-quality flashcards to make your revision sessions more effective. With built-in Pomodoro timers, Cleverly helps students stay focused and manage their time productively. Alternate between focused work intervals and short breaks to optimize learning and avoid burnout. Whether you're preparing for exams or reviewing lecture notes, Cleverly streamlines the process, making studying smarter, not harder.
